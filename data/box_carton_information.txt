CARTONS are boxes

CP = carrier pack (the moving company packs the boxes)

PBO = packed by owner

6.5 (or 6.0) means the size of the box - 6.5 (or 6.0) cubic feet which is an Extra Large box

4.1 or 4.5 is a Large box

3.1 or 3.0 is a Medium box

1.5 is a Small box or Book carton

Factor refers to the phrase "we will use a 'factor' of x lbs per packed 1.5 box", etc.
